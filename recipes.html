Indian Chinese Food Recipes

1. Vegetable Manchurian

Ingredients: Vegetables (cabbage, carrots, capsicum), All-purpose flour, Cornflour, Ginger-garlic paste, Soy sauce, Vinegar, Salt, Pepper, Sugar

Method: Grate and mix vegetables with flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, salt, pepper, and water. Make small balls and deep fry until golden. In a pan, sauté ginger-garlic paste, add soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and water. Thicken the sauce, add fried balls, toss, and serve hot.

2. Chilli Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken (boneless), Bell peppers, Onions, Ginger, Garlic, Green chilies, Soy sauce, Chili sauce, Cornflour, Oil

Method: Marinate chicken in soy sauce, cornflour, salt, and pepper. Deep fry until crisp and golden. In a pan, sauté ginger, garlic, green chilies, add chopped veggies, sauces, and fried chicken. Toss well, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.

3. Hakka Noodles

Ingredients: Noodles, Vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers), Soy sauce, Vinegar, Oil, Salt, Pepper

Method: Boil noodles, drain, and rinse with cold water. In a pan, stir-fry veggies, add noodles, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Toss well, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.

4. Szechwan Fried Rice

Ingredients: Cooked rice, Vegetables (carrots, beans, capsicum), Szechwan sauce, Garlic, Ginger, Oil

Method: In a pan, sauté garlic, ginger, and veggies. Add cooked rice, Szechwan sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry until well combined, garnish, and serve.

5. Sweet Corn Soup

Ingredients: Sweet corn, Vegetable stock, Cornflour, Spring onions, Salt, Pepper

Method: Blend sweet corn with stock, strain. Boil the strained liquid, add cornflour slurry, salt, and pepper. Simmer until thickened, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.

6. Gobi Manchurian

Ingredients: Cauliflower florets, All-purpose flour, Cornflour, Soy sauce, Ginger-garlic paste, Green chilies, Onion, Capsicum, Spring onions, Salt, Pepper

Method: Coat cauliflower with flour, cornflour, salt, pepper, and deep fry until crisp. In a pan, sauté ginger-garlic paste, add chopped onions, green chilies, capsicum, soy sauce. Toss fried cauliflower, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.

7. Chilli Paneer

Ingredients: Paneer cubes, Bell peppers, Onions, Ginger, Garlic, Green chilies, Soy sauce, Chili sauce, Cornflour, Oil

Method: Marinate paneer in soy sauce, cornflour, salt, and pepper. Deep fry until golden. In a pan, sauté ginger, garlic, green chilies, add chopped veggies, sauces, and fried paneer. Toss well, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.

8. Fried Wontons

Ingredients: Wonton wrappers, Minced chicken or vegetables, Soy sauce, Ginger, Garlic, Oil, Salt, Pepper

Method: Fill wonton wrappers with a mixture of minced chicken or vegetables seasoned with soy sauce, ginger, garlic, salt, and pepper. Seal and deep fry until golden and crispy. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
